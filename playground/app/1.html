Discovery

To us, Brand Discovery is essentially just getting to know you and establishing a game plan. Clients generally come to us in one of two ways. 1) They come to us seeking a specific deliverable: “I need a website,” or 2) They come to us knowing they need better branding or design, but don’t know where to begin or what pieces to start with.

The brand discovery process allows us to determine what you need, in what order we should deliver those items to be most effective, and create a plan of action.
